About Us

CARING IS OUR CALLING

Dr. Ugo, DDS, is the owner and family dentist in Louisville, KY. Omnis Dental was founded on the belief that everyone deserves exceptional dental health. We are here to help you achieve it. With more than 25 years of experience, Dr. Ugo is committed to providing honest and ethical treatment, taking ample time with each patient to explain every treatment in detail. His goal is to alleviate his patients’ pain or discomfort, allowing everyone to leave the practice happy and smiling.

Dr. Matthew Ugo DDS

Dr. Matthew Ugo is a long-time resident of the Louisville City area and has been providing comfortable, premier dentistry for over a decade. He is proud to put his expertise to work for patients and incorporate the latest developments in dentistry into treatment. When he isn’t working or traveling, he enjoys fitness, and reading. Education R DDS – UofL School of Dentistry R BS Biology, University of Louisville, KY
